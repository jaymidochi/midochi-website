title: Alignment to pilot process
---

How the process looks from alignment to pilot.

| Step | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| You are deciding if ABM makes sense | To fast track this, book a meeting with me |
| Discovery call + bonus | We talk through your setup, your biggest revenue challenges, and whether ABM fits right now. (Bonus) I show you a couple of relevant playbooks |
| Sign the MNDA | We sign a mutual NDA first, so you can share real data without worry |
| Alignment kickoff | You send over CRM exports: closed-won and closed-lost deals from the last 12-24 months |
| Deal and cluster analysis | We dig into those deals to find your cluster, your ICP, and qualification criteria |
| Account prioritization workshop | Together, we segment and score your account list by real probability of becoming a deal |
| Pricing | We align on the pricing and the scope of the pilot |
| Pilot plan and proposal (week 2) | We hand you a concrete plan: cluster, goal, team, timeline, and what success looks like |
| Internal alignment (week 2) | You bring sales and leadership in, using the materials we've built to get buy-in |
| Pilot kickoff call | Your pilot team meets ours, roles get assigned, and week one gets mapped out |
