title: What you get
---

Over six months, I build and hand over:

**1. The intelligence layer.** A live feed of newly incorporated family offices in Singapore: principals, directors, and the corporate-services firms behind them, named and piped to you. You see who's landing.

**2. Gatekeeper mapping.** The firms, secretaries, and advisors who control access, plus the sequence to reach them.

**3. A live corporate-services partnership.** At least one working referral partnership with a firm that already serves family-office clients.

**4. Curated sessions.** Small-group sessions of 8 to 10 people with partner firms, with a 1:1 debrief built in. The workshop motion you already run, aimed higher.

**5. Full handover.** At month six you own all of it. The data sources, the maps, the partnership, the process. I step out. Nothing stays on my side.
