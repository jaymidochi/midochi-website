---
title: 7. Phased execution
---

**Phase 1: Discovery and architecture (Weeks 1 to 3)**

Intelligence layer: map newly incorporated Singapore family offices, principals, nominee directors, and corporate secretaries by name and structure. We deliver an initial validated batch of 20 family offices first, so you can see the data quality and fit, before the full database of approximately 750 is released in stages across the engagement. Define the ideal client profile with Dr Shirin. Build the gatekeeper map. Develop positioning and messaging that frames Dr Shirin as the authority on preventative and predictive health for globally mobile families. Design the webinar and shared-session concept.

**Phase 2: Deployment (Weeks 4 to 16)**

Research-led outreach to gatekeepers (corporate services firms, EAs, wealth advisors, lifestyle managers). Open corporate-services partnerships. Run curated small-group sessions and webinars positioned as education, not sales. Parallel engagement across every stakeholder inside a target account. Follow-up sequences that move contacts from cold to warm to booked.

**Phase 3: Optimisation and handover (Weeks 17 to 24)**

Iterate on messaging and targeting from live results. Document every SOP and playbook. Train Dr Shirin's team to run the system. Transfer the full channel into her hands.
