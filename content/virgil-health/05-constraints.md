title: 4. Constraints
---

- **Regulatory:** an American-trained physician advising Singapore-resident patients needs to confirm registration requirements with the Singapore Medical Council. The channel is built in parallel and does not depend on this, but member conversion for SG-resident patients does. *(Client to confirm registration pathway; Midochi to support with what we can verify.)*
- **Geographic:** Dr Shirin is based in Switzerland; all delivery is virtual. The channel is designed around remote consultation.
- **Bandwidth:** small team. The system is built to be run by a lean in-house operator after handover.
